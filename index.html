I am a 4th year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science, the University of Hong Kong (HKU). Before joining HKU, I received a Bachelor of Science from Sun Yat-sen University in 2009. Research Interests

I'm interested in solving computer vision applications by optimization and machine learning. These applications include image segmentation, scene understanding, image retrieval and so on. Especially, I focus on the following topics:

  • Markov random fields and conditional random fields
  • Structure prediction
  • Metric learning
  • Multimodality analysis
  • Hashing
